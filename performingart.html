Did You Know?

Theater Fact

Ethiopia has one of Africa's oldest theater traditions, with roots in religious ceremonies dating back to the 4th century.

Music Insight

The krar, a traditional Ethiopian lyre, has been played for over 3,000 years and is mentioned in ancient Greek texts.

Dance History

Eskista, the iconic Ethiopian shoulder dance, is considered one of the most complex African dance forms.

Performance Tip

Breathing from the diaphragm can increase vocal projection by 300% without straining your voice.
