Our Art Disciplines

Visual Arts

Explore painting, drawing, sculpture, and digital art with professional artists. Our program emphasizes both traditional techniques and contemporary practices.

  • Oil & acrylic painting
  • Charcoal & pencil drawing
  • Ceramics & sculpture
  • Digital illustration

Performing Arts

Develop your talents in drama, dance, and music through our comprehensive performance program with regular showcases.

  • Theater production
  • Contemporary dance
  • Traditional Ethiopian music
  • Stagecraft & design

Digital Arts

Master the tools of modern creative expression through digital media and design.

  • Graphic design
  • 3D modeling
  • Animation
  • Video production
