Our Story

At Educate Ethiopia, we believe that involvement in education is vital. Whether we are parents, community activists, teachers, principals, or decision makers, we all have a critical role to play in improving Ethiopia's education system.

Our decisions and actions affect the education, health, and welfare of our children. By continuing to develop and promote the use of Educate Ethiopia, we are committed to doing what we can to provide parents across the country with information and analysis to help them make better-informed decisions about their child's education.

Educate Ethiopia is the BIGGEST and MOST active Online School Directory in Ethiopia! We have been in operation since 2008, and we were the first online schools database in Ethiopia.

Our platform bridges the gap between parents and educational institutions, creating a transparent ecosystem where information flows freely and decisions are made with confidence.

Our Purpose

Mission

To provide parents with up to date, relevant information and tools so they can choose the best school for their children, support their children's education, and improve schools in their local communities.

Vision

Educate Ethiopia aims to be the single largest guide for all schools in Ethiopia with both public and private schools, creating a transformative impact on the nation's education landscape.

Values

Transparency, Accessibility, Community, Innovation, and Excellence guide everything we do. We believe every child deserves access to quality education information.
